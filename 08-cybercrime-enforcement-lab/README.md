# 08 - Enforcement & Sanctioning Issues of Cybercrime

This section demonstrates enforcement and sanctioning issues in cybercrime investigations using a lab focused on forensic software tools such as Autopsy, FTK Imager Lite, and WinHex. The report highlights proper evidence handling, adherence to chain-of-command procedures, and compliance with legal standards to ensure that digital evidence is admissible in court.
